Welcome at Ticq. We are providing services to create the best application and (internal) developer platforms for your organisation. Specialised in container platforms at the core and experienced in developer team practices. Connecting and integrating DevOps Engineers and teams into a common platform to improve and speed up your organizational capability of delivering towards your production environments and your business goals.

Consultancy

Need someone from the outside to assess your current state of your platform engineering initiative? Or help brainstorming about how this initiative would look like? Or help management convince a platform is needed? We are capable of doing this on a developer level as well as C-level with experience on both.

Training
