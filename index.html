Why Ticq?

Platform engineering consultancies are not all the same. Here is what makes working with Ticq different.

Independent advice

No vendor partnerships, no reseller agreements. Recommendations are based solely on what fits your team, stack, and context.

You work with the expert

Gijs does the work directly. No account managers, no juniors staffed on the engagement after the pitch. The person you speak to is the person doing the work.

Knowledge transfer by design

Every engagement is structured so your team can operate independently when it ends. The goal is to leave your organisation more capable, not more dependent.

Pragmatic over prescriptive

No rigid methodologies imposed on your team. The approach adapts to your constraints, culture, and pace — starting from where you actually are, not where a framework says you should be.

About Ticq

Ticq Software Engineering was founded in 2024 by Gijs van Dulmen, driven by a passion for the full software delivery lifecycle. The company helps teams and organisations design, build, and operate high-quality platforms — from CI/CD pipelines to production runtime environments.

Gijs brings hands-on experience building platforms that serve over 50 development teams, across both CI/CD and production runtime environments. He specialises in container platforms, developer experience, and helping organisations scale their engineering capability.
